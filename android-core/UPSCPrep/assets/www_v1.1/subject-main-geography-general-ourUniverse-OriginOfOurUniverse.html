BIG BANG THEORY is the concept that explains the birth and the expansion of the Universe. According to it, Scientists believed that before 15 billion years ago, dust particles in the universe get compressed and collision between the matter lead to the explosion. This explosion is known as Big Bang. This explosion lead to the expansion of the Universe and in the formation of Stars, Galaxies and other more cosmic particles.
