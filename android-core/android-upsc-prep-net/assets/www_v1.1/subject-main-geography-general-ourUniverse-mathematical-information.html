Light Year
Distance covered by Light travelling One year in a Vaccum (9.46 X 1012 Kms.)
Astronomical Unit (AU)
The Mean distance between the Sun and the Earth (1.49 x 108 km). 1 light year = 60,000 AU.
Cosmic Year/ Galactic Year
The Timeperiod of revolution of Sun around the galactic centre (250 million years).
Parsec
Distance at which the mean radius of the Earth's orbit subtends an angle of one second of an arc. 1 parsec = 3.26 light years.
